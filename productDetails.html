The Sonoma Coast's rocky, well drained terrain and elevated proximity to the cool Pacific Ocean allow us to grow Pinot Noir grapes with fresh, bright red fruit, complex minerality, and coastal acidity. Inviting and expressive aromas of bright red cherry, and raspberry are combined with hints of savory ground clove spice and graham cracker. Nuances of graphite and sea salt bring another dimension that come together with a bright acidity driving a long, lively finish.
